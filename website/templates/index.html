About

A problem I've always had when looking at a music festival lineup was trying to search all the artists I didn't know on Spotify to see what type of genres will be played at the festival. This app is an easy way to see all the genres that will be playing at your festival. There is also a feature to log into your spotify account and make a playlist with all the top songs from the artists that will be playing. Just upload a file or search for a festival, then you have the option to filter the results by genre and change the amount of songs per artist. Then click the "Make Spotify Playlist" button to log in and make your playlist.

The backend of this project is made coded with Python and uses Spotify and ChatGPT's APIs to find artists/make playlists and to find lineups using AI, respectively. This project was a way for me to get familiar with full stack web development using Flask. I learned a lot while developing this website and I plan on adding many more features once I have the time.

Future features I plan on adding:
